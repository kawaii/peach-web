How to Join
Step 1 Use the Discord invite link at the bottom of the page to join our small, private server.
Step 2 In the #bot-commands channel, use the command ?rank minecraft to get access to the #minecraft-chat channel where you can request to be added to the whitelist.
Step 3 We are a small, private community. Acceptance onto the whitelist depends on an existing member vouching for your good character and willingness to play by our house rules.
